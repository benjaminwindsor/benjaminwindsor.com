Drop the planets is a concept apparel brand that I created for my third year dissertation project. This project included a website, many different t-shirt designs created using Illustrator and Photoshop, a big cartel store, social media pages, logos, slogan development and the complete brand identity. A photoshoot with two models showcased the t-shirts and merchandise. Which was then compiled into a large presentation.
